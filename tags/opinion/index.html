Why is Salesforce Industries a big deal?

Changing the way we build Salesforce Solutions

Salesforce is bringing in new tools to help businesses achieve quicker value while reducing the cost to build solutions. Salesforce is using industry specific clouds that add to the Salesforce platform with smart defaults which help propel solutions forward. Salesforce started (and still advertises itself) as a CRM platform on the cloud. Over the years, Salesforce has acquired new tools and companies that help extend its margin of innovation from its competition.
